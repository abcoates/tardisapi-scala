TARDIS for GOR tracks symptoms of gastro-oesophageal reflux in children.

Existing Users Login
New users Register

To register as a new user, here's what you need to do:

  1. Review the information for participants.
  2. Sign up on this website.
  3. Download the TARDIS for GOR app from the App store

When you register, we will ask you for basic information on the person you would like to track i.e. their date of birth and their gender.

When you register, we will email you a unique study id which will help us recognise your data.

After you download the TARDIS app from the app store, you will use this unique study id to activate your app.

The data from your phone will be sent back to us securely and anonymously.
