TARDIS for GOR tracks symptoms of
gastro-oesophageal reflux in children.

Existing Users Click to login
New users Click to register

To register as a new user, here's what you need to do:

  1. Review the information for participants.
  2. Sign up on this website.
  3. Download the TARDIS for GOR app from the iPhone App store

Please note:

  • When you register, we will ask you for basic information about the person whose reflux you are tracking i.e. their date of birth and their gender.
  • After you register, we will email you a unique study id which will help us recognise your data.
  • After you download the TARDIS app from the iPhone App store, you will use this unique study id to activate your app.
  • The reflux data that you enter into your phone will be sent back to us securely and anonymously.
